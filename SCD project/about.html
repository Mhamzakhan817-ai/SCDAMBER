About Global Pathways Travel

We design comfortable, memorable journeys across Pakistan – from the valleys of Skardu and Naran to the culture of Lahore, the palaces of Bahawalpur, the hills of Muree and the forests of Ziarat.

Our Story

Global Pathways Travel was created from a simple idea: travellers should be able to experience the real beauty of Pakistan without worrying about the details. We bring together comfortable transport, handpicked hotels and trusted local partners so that you can focus on enjoying the journey.

From our base in Pakistan, we specialise in small‑group and private tours to the country’s most loved destinations – Skardu, Naran, Lahore, Bahawalpur, Muree and Ziarat. Whether it’s your first time visiting the north or you already know these places well, we help you see them from a fresh perspective.

Every itinerary we design is based on real on‑ground experience. We know which roads are comfortable, when the weather is best, and how to balance travel time with sightseeing and rest.

Why Travel With Us?

We focus on comfort, safety and local expertise so that your trip is smooth from the first message until you return home.

Our itineraries are based on real driving times and local conditions, with enough time for photos, rest and unplanned stops.

Flexible for Your Group

Destinations We Cover

We currently focus on key regions across Pakistan, with the flexibility to create custom itineraries that combine multiple destinations.

Skardu & Northern Valleys

Lakes, high peaks and dramatic landscapes – ideal for adventure‑oriented travellers who still want comfortable stays.

Naran & Kaghan Valley

Alpine scenery, rivers and lakes that are perfect for first‑time visitors to Pakistan’s north.

Lahore & Cultural Highlights

Mughal architecture, old bazaars and some of the best food in the country – a must‑visit for culture lovers.

Bahawalpur & Cholistan Desert

Royal palaces, historic forts and vast desert landscapes, showing another side of Pakistan.

Muree & Nearby Hills

Classic hill‑station escape for families and groups who want cooler weather and easy access from big cities.

Ziarat & Balochistan

Calm juniper forests and simple mountain life – perfect for travellers looking for peace and fresh air.
