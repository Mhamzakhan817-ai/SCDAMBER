Transportation Services

Comfortable, safe and reliable vehicles for your journeys across Pakistan – from airport pickups to full tour transport for your group.

Our Vehicles

We work with experienced drivers and well‑maintained vehicles that match your group size and route. Below are examples of the types of transport we can arrange. Actual vehicle models may vary depending on availability and city.

Private Car (Sedan)

1–3 travellers · Light luggage

Ideal for couples or very small groups who want maximum comfort and flexibility on shorter routes – airport transfers, city tours and day trips.

  • Air‑conditioned sedan with experienced driver
  • Flexible stops for photos, tea and meals
  • Great for Lahore city tours and short inter‑city journeys

Hiace / Coaster

8–18 travellers · Group tours

Comfortable choice for families and medium‑sized groups on longer routes such as Lahore–Naran, Lahore–Skardu (via road) or custom itineraries.

  • Air‑conditioned, with good leg room
  • Used for most of our group departures
  • Luggage space adapted to your group size

4x4 Jeep

4–6 travellers · Off‑road routes

Required on rough or steep roads, especially around Skardu, Deosai Plains and some lake access roads in Naran and Kaghan Valley.

  • Local drivers familiar with mountain tracks
  • Used for Saif‑ul‑Malook, Deosai and similar routes
  • Can be added to your package only for specific days

SUV / Executive

3–5 travellers · Extra comfort

For guests who prefer more legroom and a smoother ride, especially on longer drives between cities and towards the north.

  • Higher ground clearance for mixed road conditions
  • Comfortable seats and better suspension
  • Recommended for private small‑group tours

Tourist Bus

20–40 travellers · Large groups

Suitable for corporate trips, university groups or large families travelling together on main highways and accessible routes.

  • Comfortable seating with AC
  • Used mainly on main inter‑city highways
  • Local jeeps can be added for areas where buses cannot go

Airport & City Transfers

Individual or group pickups

Pre‑arranged transfers from airports and bus terminals to your hotel or directly to the start of your tour.

  • Available in major cities such as Lahore, Islamabad and Karachi
  • Can be arranged as part of a package or as a stand‑alone service
  • Ideal for late‑night or early‑morning arrivals

How Transportation Works

1

Share your plan

Tell us your dates, destinations and number of travellers via the contact form or WhatsApp.

2

We suggest vehicles

We recommend the right combination of vehicles based on route, comfort and budget (car, Hiace, jeep, bus).

3

Confirm & travel

Once confirmed, your vehicles and drivers are booked and ready to accompany you throughout the trip.

Note: Road conditions and weather can change quickly in some regions. We always try to use the most comfortable option available, but in remote areas (especially in the north) rougher roads and jeeps may be required for safety.
